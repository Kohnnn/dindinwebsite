*Prepared by Mindshare*^^

### 1. Background & Problem Statement

* **In 2023, Huggies experienced slower growth compared to the overall category, which grew by 18%**^^.
* **Despite holding a 28% market share, the brand trailed behind its main competitor, Bobby, and faced increasing pressure from hand-carried products**^^.
* **Huggies was a latecomer to the social commerce landscape**^^. **While competitors explored TikTok Shop in 2023, Huggies had not yet established an official store on the platform**^^.
* **During this period, social commerce gained significant momentum; the Mom & Baby category saw a 48% GMV growth on TikTok Shop, with affiliates contributing 47% of that GMV**^^.
* **Strategic Shift:** To recapture market share, Huggies made a bold decision to prioritize digital commerce investments, reallocating substantial budgets from traditional branding to become the top brand in digital commerce sales^^.

### 2. Marketing Objectives & Challenges

To deliver on the client's expectations, the team faced two core challenges:

* **E-Commerce (Shopee, Lazada, Tiki):** Achieve `<span class="citation-467">VND464bn</span>` in Paid GMV, representing an 80% increase versus 2023, while securing a higher ROAS (+25% vs 2023)^^^^^^^^.
* **TikTok Shop:** Build a top-tier presence from the ground up, targeting `<span class="citation-466">VND52bn</span>` in Paid GMV with a strict ROAS requirement of 8.5^^. **The ultimate goal was to exceed the revenue of the top incumbent brand in the category**^^.

### 3. Results & Business Impact

The campaign successfully turned the brand's digital presence around in 2024:

* **Revenue Growth:** Achieved `<span class="citation-464">VND608bn</span>` in Paid GMV, a 135% increase compared to 2023^^.
* **Platform Leadership:** Maintained the top position across pure e-commerce players (Shopee, Lazada, Tiki), growing Paid GMV by 103% year-over-year^^. **By August, Huggies also became the highest-grossing Baby Diaper brand on TikTok Shop**^^.
* **Profitability Uplift:** Total e-commerce and social commerce ROAS improved by 28%^^. **Pure e-commerce ROAS grew by an impressive 58% year-over-year**^^.
* **Social Commerce Acceleration:** Achieved continuous month-over-month growth on TikTok Shop, culminating in a 231% Paid GMV increase in H2 versus H1^^.
* **Premiumization:** Successfully captured `<span class="citation-458">VND184bn</span>` in Paid GMV from premium lines (Nature Made and Skin Perfect), which contributed 30% to total Paid GMV compared to just 14% in 2023^^.
* **Bonus Secured:** The team successfully gained a USD36,000 bonus for achieving the highest GMV target milestone^^.

### 4. Insights & Strategic Approach

The strategy was grounded in several key consumer insights:

* **Channel Shifts:** Shopping behavior is heavily shifting online, with e-commerce and Mom & Baby stores being the only growing retail channels^^.
* **The Rise of Shoppertainment:** "Shoppertainment" has exploded in Vietnam via TikTok Shop and Shopee^^.
* **Targeting First-Time Moms (Gen Z):** Capturing first-time mothers is highly lucrative due to brand inertia; once they find a suitable diaper, they rarely switch^^. **Gen Z has a high demographic match rate (estimated higher than 87%) with first-time mothers**^^.

**Two-Pronged Strategic Approach:**

1. **Enhance E-Commerce Dominance:** Maintain leadership on Shopee, Lazada, and Tiki by maximizing visibility and creating new sales occasions^^^^^^^^.
2. **Accelerate on TikTok Shop:** Prioritize rapid growth on the platform to capture the Gen Z first-time mom demographic^^^^^^^^.

### 5. Ideas & Execution

#### Strengthening the E-Commerce Core (Shopee)

* **Super Huggies Day:** To combat low traffic volumes during business-as-usual (BAU) days, the team created "Super Huggies Day" to drive incremental GMV between major Mega and Payday campaigns^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^.
* **Portfolio Management:** Implemented strategic ad format investments across two separate storefronts (Huggies Official Store and Huggies Viet Nam) to ensure comprehensive ad coverage while minimizing SKU cannibalization^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^.
* **Tactical Timing:** Capitalized on buying patterns by optimizing ad spend. **Data showed Mega campaigns peak from 0 to 2 a.m., while Super Huggies Day conversions surge late in the evening**^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^.
* **Offsite Traffic Generation:** Leveraged Meta (CPAS/Livestream ads), Criteo (Dynamic ads), and Google Ads (Search) to funnel high-intent customers directly to the Huggies store^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^.

#### Accelerating on TikTok Shop

* **Rapid Ascent:** Transformed Huggies from a latecomer into a front-runner within 8 months^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^.
* **Hero SKU:** Positioned Huggies Skin Perfect as the best-selling SKU on the platform, contributing 43% of the monthly GMV^^.
* **Traffic & Conversion Funnel:** * *H1 2024 (Build Phase):* Focused on building store and channel traffic by targeting broad audiences using TikTok 3rd party data^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^.
  * *H2 2024 (Scale Phase):* Utilized built-up traffic to scale GMV via retargeting and lookalike audiences (LAL)^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^.
* **Budget Optimization (H2 2024):**
  * *LSA (31% of budget):* Allocated 45% to Prospecting (Broad, LAL) and 55% to Retargeting^^^^^^^^.
  * *VSA (34% of budget):* Allocated 40% to Prospecting and 60% to Retargeting^^^^^^^^.
  * *PSA (35% of budget):* Shifted heavily toward conversion, allocating 60-70% to LAL/Retargeting and 30-40% to Broad audiences^^^^^^^^.

**Massive Conversion Leakage Across the Mobile Purchase Journey **^^

* **Brand.com is experiencing critical user drop-off specifically on mobile devices**^^.
* **The current purchase journey requires up to 12 steps to complete a transaction, severely degrading completion rates**^^.
* **Key Telemetry (GA Data, Aug 1 - Aug 23)**^^:
  * **90.83% drop-off from Product View to Add to Cart (A2C)**^^.
  * **85.6% drop-off from A2C to Purchase completion**^^.
  * **66.03% critical drop-off specifically localized at the Identification/Login step**^^.
* **The "Leaky Funnel" illustrates a 90.83% drop-off right after Product View, leading into a 66.03% critical failure leakage point at the Checkout (Identification) stage**^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^.

---

**Autopsy: High Cognitive Load and UI Friction Force User Abandonment **^^^^^^^^

* 1. **Dead-End UI: Promotional banners lack direct product hyperlinks**^^. **Clicking primary campaign imagery results in no action, instantly killing momentum**^^. **This manifests as unclickable areas on campaign banners**^^.
* 2. **Hard Friction (The Wall): Forced login and account creation act as a massive barrier, driving the 66.03% drop**^^. **Users encounter mandatory account creation with no guest checkout option**^^.
* 3. **Broken Social: Missing Facebook Graph API traps users in an authentication loop, exhausting motivation**^^. **Attempting to use social autofill results in browser loops**^^^^^^^^.

---

**Convenience is King: Checkout Friction is Killing Purchase Intent **^^

* **Weight vs. Motivation:** As users move through Discovery, Consideration, Cart, and Identification, increasing friction steadily decreases their motivation^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^. **The critical drop occurs below the purchase threshold precisely at the forced account creation/no guest checkout stage**^^^^^^^^.
* **The Reality:** Modern mobile users prioritize speed and seamless execution above all else^^.
* **The Flaw:** Our current architecture actively penalizes high-intent buyers by prioritizing internal data collection over transaction execution^^.
* **The Opportunity:** Eliminating forced identification recaptures a massive portion of the 85.6% of users who display intent but abandon prior to payment^^.
* **Key Takeaway:** "We are asking for marriage before the first date. Data collection must follow, not precede, the transaction." ^^

---

**Streamlining the Path to Purchase (12 Steps → 6 Steps) **^^

**UX/UI Overhaul Requirements**^^

* **Deploy true Guest Checkout (Remove forced login)**^^.
* **Embed direct product tracking links in all promotional banners**^^.
* **Anchor sticky, high-contrast CTA buttons (ATC, Purchase) to the mobile viewport bottom**^^.

**Current State - Friction (12 Steps)**^^The current 12-step flow is bloated with bottlenecks and friction points: Home Page ^^-> Category Page ^^-> Product Page (Bottleneck) ^^^^^^^^-> Add to Cart ^^-> Cart View ^^-> Identification (Bottleneck) ^^-> Shipping Info (Friction Point) ^^^^^^^^-> Shipping Method ^^-> Payment Method (Friction Point) ^^-> Review Order ^^-> Confirm Order ^^-> Order Placed^^.

**Future State - Flow (6 Steps)**^^The streamlined flow accelerates the user directly to checkout: Promotion Page ^^-> Add to Cart ^^-> Cart Review ^^-> Simplified Guest Checkout ^^-> Payment ^^-> Complete^^.

**Comparison Matrix**^^

| **Metric**         | **Before**       | **After**             |
| ------------------------ | ---------------------- | --------------------------- |
| **Steps**          | **12**           | **6**                 |
| **Authentication** | **Forced Login** | **Optional/Guest**    |
| **Promotions**     | **Dead UI**      | **Clickable Routing** |

**Execution Roadmap: Fixing the Funnel **^^

**Phase 1: Quick Wins (Weeks 1-2)**^^Categorized as High Impact / Low Effort ^^

* **Attach dynamic hyperlinks to all active promotional banners**^^.
* **Increase size, contrast, and hit-area of ATC and Purchase CTAs**^^.

**Phase 2: Mid-Term (Weeks 3-4)**^^

* **Simplify the cart review flow**^^.
* **Consolidate Shipping Information and Payment Methods onto a single screen to reduce page loads**^^.

**Phase 3: Advanced (Weeks 5-6)**^^

* **Implement true Guest Checkout architecture**^^.
